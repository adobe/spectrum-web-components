Form-associated custom element PoC: combobox

Research artifact for ElementInternals and FACE patterns applied to a combobox-shaped control (text input + listbox). This version has no cross-root IDREF workarounds — it shows what ElementInternals does out of the box.

Configuration: Open face-combobox.js and toggle USE_INTERNALS_ROLE (line 17) between true / false to compare behavior with and without internals.role = 'combobox' on the host element.
